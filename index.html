Our Journey

Love Story

The Song

It started with a guitar and a song neither of us can remember the title of anymore — just the chords, and the way Dyan hummed along like she'd known it her whole life. Yus kept playing that same song every chance he got after that, half hoping she'd walk by again.

The Hallways

High school hallways turned into long talks after class. Then came the food trips — jeepney rides to nowhere in particular, split meals because neither wanted to admit they were low on allowance, and that one carinderia we still can't drive past without pointing and saying "remember that time..."

The Quiet Jamming

Years passed. The guitar got old, the song got forgotten, but the jamming never really stopped — it just turned into something quieter. Late-night talks. Shared plates. Showing up, again and again, for the small ordinary days that somehow added up to a whole life together.

From one song to a thousand food trips, to forever.
